    About Me

    Hello and welcome to all things, Melody. Currently, I am enrolled in a Full-Stack Web Development bootcamp through the University of MN. It is a 6 month high-intensity program that will educate and bring me up-to-date on everything necessary to work in either front-end or back-end coding careers. Within this program I am learning HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, React.js, Node.js, Database Theory, MongoDB, MySQL, Command Line, Web Apps, Git, and more.

    I have a long history in customer service as well as 6 years working in vendor management. My experience gives me the knowledge to both assess the needs of clients, as well as plan and execute the actions needed to assist them with their goals. I hope to work with a company where I can continue to grow my dev skills and be mentored. An ideal workplace has a dedicated dev team that is continually updating an innovating the applications used exclusively by that company.

    I have a passion for back-end coding, especially JavaScript and jQuery. I look forward to all the new things I have yet to learn!

    I am particularly proud of my contribution to the Snaccidental Recipe project. There was a lot of tricky JavaScript/jQuery coding that I learned and executed to make the project function as well as it does.
